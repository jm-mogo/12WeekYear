Why I made this site

I’ve set a lot of goals in my life, and even when I’ve accomplished some of them in the past, those last years I haven’t been that well organized with my priorities. So, this year I set some goals (actually last year goals). But this time I’m committed to accomplish them. It’s the second month of the year and though I’ve advanced, I could still do better, I could focus more, and get things done.

Well, this week I found a method to set goals, it’s called “12-week year”. I read the book in two days, and I’m ready to start next week. I want to document all the process, so that I can see all my progress and where I can improve. And share it with you, whether now or in the future.
